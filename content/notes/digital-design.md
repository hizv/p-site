## Digital {#digital}


### Advantages {#advantages}

-   Easier to design, using switching circuits (only **HIGH** and **LOW**)
-   Storage
-   Accuracy and precision
-   Programmable
-   Less affected by noise


### Limitation {#limitation}

Real world is mainly analog
